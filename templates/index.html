Welcome to Notebox DEV!

This application allows you to upload audio files, get them transcribed, and then receive an AI-generated summary, key ideas, and Q&A pairs based on the transcription.

Use the navigation bar to upload a new note or view existing notes.

How it Works:

  • Upload: Go to the "Upload Note" page and select an audio file (e.g., WAV, MP3).
  • Transcription: The audio is sent to Deepgram for fast and accurate transcription.
  • AI Processing: The transcript is then sent to DeepSeek to:
    • Generate a concise summary.
    • Extract key ideas.
    • Create potential question & answer pairs.
  • Review: View your processed notes, including the transcript, summary, and Q&A, on the "View Notes" page. You can also ask further questions about your notes.
